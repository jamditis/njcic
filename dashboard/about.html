NJCIC Social Media Impact Dashboard

Tracking and analyzing the social media presence of New Jersey Civic Information Consortium grantees to measure civic journalism impact across the state.

What is NJCIC?

The New Jersey Civic Information Consortium (NJCIC) is a first-in-the-nation initiative that provides funding to support local journalism and civic information projects across New Jersey. Established in 2018, the Consortium is a partnership between six public research universities in the state.

NJCIC awards grants to news organizations, community groups, and civic technology projects that are working to address local news deserts and information gaps in New Jersey communities.

Member Universities

  • Montclair State University (host)
  • Rutgers University
  • Rowan University
  • New Jersey Institute of Technology
  • The College of New Jersey
  • Kean University

About This Dashboard

This dashboard provides real-time analytics on the social media performance of NJCIC grantees. By tracking engagement across platforms like TikTok, Bluesky, YouTube, and more, we aim to:

  • Measure Impact - Quantify the reach and engagement of civic journalism projects
  • Identify Trends - Understand which platforms and content types resonate with audiences
  • Support Grantees - Provide data-driven insights to help organizations improve their outreach
  • Demonstrate Value - Show funders and stakeholders the tangible impact of their investment

Dashboard Features

Individual Grantee Pages

Detailed analytics for each grantee including platform breakdown, top posts, and engagement trends.

Comparison Tools

Compare grantees side-by-side to understand relative performance across metrics.

Platform Analytics

Cross-platform comparisons showing which platforms drive the most engagement.

Weekly Updates

Data is automatically refreshed weekly to provide up-to-date insights.
